About

Built for learners
who mean it.

Study Planet is a personal learning tracker that helps you build consistency, measure progress, and stay motivated — one session at a time.

Whether you're studying for exams, learning a new language, or building a career skill — Study Planet gives you a structured space to track what you learn, how long you studied, and how hard it felt.

With streaks, points, and an AI coach, it turns studying from a chore into a habit you actually enjoy.
